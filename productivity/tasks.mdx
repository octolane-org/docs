---
title: "Tasks workflows"
description: "Daily task management, snoozing, and task views."
---

- **Today view**. What's due today across all your records.
- **Snooze**. Push a task to a specific date and time.
- **Recurring tasks**. Daily, weekly, custom cadences.
- **Auto-generated**. Meetings and emails create tasks for you.
- **Assignment**. Assign to teammates with @mention.
