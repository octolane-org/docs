---
title: "Views and saved filters"
description: "Slice your data, save the view, share with your team."
---

A view is a saved combination of filter + columns + sort + grouping on any object.

- Personal views are private.
- Workspace views are shared with the team.
- Pin views to your sidebar.

Common views: "My open deals", "Deals closing this quarter", "No activity in 14 days", "New accounts this week".
