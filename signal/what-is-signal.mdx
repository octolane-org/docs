Signal is Octolane's website visitor intelligence feature. It identifies which companies are visiting your website in real-time - even if those visitors never fill out a form, start a chat, or leave any contact information.

## How it works

When someone visits your website, Signal:

1. **Identifies the company** they work for using IP intelligence and behavioral signals
2. **Enriches the company record** with firmographic data - size, industry, location, funding, tech stack
3. **Finds key contacts** at that company - decision-makers and relevant stakeholders
4. **Scores them** against your ideal customer profile
5. **Triggers next steps** - for qualified companies, Signal can automatically create a deal and pass the lead to the Outreach Agent

## Why it matters

Most B2B websites convert less than 2% of visitors into leads. The other 98% leave without you ever knowing they were there. Signal gives you visibility into that hidden 98%.

Instead of waiting for prospects to raise their hand, you can proactively reach out to companies that are already researching your product.

## What you see

In your Octolane dashboard, Signal shows you:

- Companies currently on your site and companies that visited recently
- Which pages they viewed, how many times they've visited, and how long they spent
- How well each company matches your ideal customer profile
- Suggested contacts at those companies for outreach
