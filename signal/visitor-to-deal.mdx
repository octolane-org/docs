Signal doesn't just identify visitors - it turns them into pipeline. When a qualified company shows up on your site, Octolane can take the entire journey from anonymous visitor to active deal without a single manual step.

## The automated flow

1. **Visitor lands on your site** - Signal identifies the company
2. **Company is qualified** - matched against your ideal customer profile
3. **Record is created** - an account and contact record are added to your CRM (or existing ones are updated)
4. **Deal is created** - an opportunity is added to your pipeline at the appropriate stage
5. **Outreach is drafted** - the Outreach Agent writes a personalized message referencing their browsing activity
6. **You approve** - the message sits in your queue until you tap approve

## What the outreach looks like

The Outreach Agent crafts messages based on real context:

- Which pages the prospect viewed (pricing page, case studies, specific features)
- How many times they've visited and how recently
- Company size, industry, and other firmographic details
- Any existing relationship signals (did someone on your team email someone at that company before?)

## Configuring the flow

You control every step:

- Whether Signal should automatically create deals for qualified visitors, or just flag them for your review
- What qualification criteria a company must meet to trigger the flow
- Whether the Outreach Agent should draft a message automatically or wait for your instruction
- The template, tone, and style of outreach messages
