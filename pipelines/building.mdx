---
title: "Building your pipeline"
description: "Stages, criteria, and probability."
---

Settings → Pipelines → New.

For each stage define:

- **Name** and color
- **Probability** (used by AI Chat forecasts)
- **Entry criteria**. Conditions that auto-advance a deal in
- **Exit criteria**. What must be true before moving on

Keep it to 5–7 stages for founder-led motions.
