AI Chat lets you talk to your CRM the way you'd talk to a colleague. Ask questions, give instructions, or get updates - in plain English.

## What you can do

**Ask questions about your pipeline:**
- "What's the latest with the Acme deal?"
- "How many deals did we close this month?"
- "Which deals haven't had activity in two weeks?"
- "Show me all contacts at companies with more than 50 employees"

**Give instructions to agents:**
- "Draft a follow-up to Sarah about the pricing discussion"
- "Send outreach to the last 10 companies that visited our pricing page"
- "Create a deal for my meeting with Stripe tomorrow"

**Get summaries and insights:**
- "Summarize my pipeline this week"
- "What should I focus on today?"
- "Which deals are at risk of going stale?"

## How it works

AI Chat has full access to your CRM data - deals, contacts, companies, emails, meetings, website visits, and agent activity. When you ask a question, it searches across everything and gives you a direct answer.

When you give an instruction, it routes the task to the right agent. For example, "Draft a follow-up to Sarah" sends the request to the Follow-Up Agent, which drafts the email and puts it in your approval queue.

## Where to find it

AI Chat is always available from the sidebar in your Octolane dashboard. Click the chat icon or use the keyboard shortcut to open it.
