The Follow-Up Agent monitors every active conversation in your pipeline. When a thread goes quiet, it drafts a follow-up email in your voice and tone - then queues it for your approval.

## What it does

The Follow-Up Agent:

1. **Monitors conversation activity** - tracks email threads, meeting follow-ups, and pending responses across your pipeline
2. **Detects stale conversations** - identifies when a prospect hasn't replied or an action item hasn't been addressed
3. **Drafts follow-up emails** - writes in your voice, preserves the subject line, includes the right context, and matches your communication style
4. **Queues for approval** - every draft shows up in your approval queue. You review, edit if needed, and approve with one tap

## Why it matters

Most deals don't die because of price or competition. They die because someone forgot to follow up. The Follow-Up Agent makes sure that never happens - not because you're more disciplined, but because the system is.

## How it writes like you

The Follow-Up Agent learns your communication style from your sent emails. It matches:

- **Tone** - casual, professional, or somewhere in between
- **Length** - short and punchy or detailed and thorough
- **Greeting and sign-off patterns** - how you start and end emails
- **Context** - it reads the full thread before drafting so the follow-up makes sense

## Configuration

You control how the Follow-Up Agent behaves:

- **Timing** - how long a conversation should be quiet before a follow-up is drafted (e.g., 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks)
- **Priority** - which deals or contacts should get follow-ups first
- **Approval level** - require approval for every follow-up, or auto-send for low-stakes conversations
