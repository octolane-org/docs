The Form Submission Agent captures every lead that fills out a form on your website. It qualifies them instantly based on your criteria and routes them to the right place in your pipeline - no manual review needed.

## What it does

When someone submits a form on your website:

1. **Captures the submission** - pulls in every field from the form (name, email, company, message, etc.)
2. **Creates a contact record** - or updates an existing one if they're already in your CRM
3. **Enriches the data** - adds company info, job title, social profiles, and more from the web
4. **Qualifies the lead** - scores them against your ideal customer profile
5. **Routes them** - places qualified leads in the right pipeline stage and can trigger outreach

## Speed to lead

The gap between when a prospect fills out a form and when someone follows up is where most deals die. The Form Submission Agent eliminates that gap. It processes submissions in real-time and can hand off qualified leads to the Follow-Up Agent or Outreach Agent immediately.

## Configuration

Set up the Form Submission Agent by defining:

- **Which forms to capture** - connect your website forms, landing pages, or third-party form tools
- **Qualification criteria** - company size, industry, job title, or any custom rule
- **Routing rules** - which pipeline stage and owner qualified leads should go to
- **Follow-up triggers** - whether to automatically draft a response for qualified leads
