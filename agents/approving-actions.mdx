Octolane agents draft emails, create deals, and suggest next steps - but nothing touches the outside world without your approval. The approval queue is your single place to review and act on everything your agents want to do.

## The approval queue

Open the approval queue from your sidebar. Each item shows:

- **Which agent** is requesting the action
- **What it wants to do** - send an email, create a deal, update a record
- **Why** - the context that triggered the action (e.g., "conversation has been quiet for 7 days")
- **The full draft** - the email text, deal details, or suggested update

## Your options

For each item, you can:

- **Approve** - the action goes through as drafted
- **Edit and approve** - make changes to the draft, then send
- **Dismiss** - reject the action. The agent learns from your decision over time.

## What goes through the queue

Actions that affect external communication always require approval:

- Emails drafted by the Follow-Up Agent
- Outreach messages drafted by the Outreach Agent
- Any response suggested by an agent

Internal actions - like creating a contact record, enriching a company, or updating a deal stage - happen automatically. You can review these in the Activity Feed at any time.

## Notification settings

Configure how you're notified about pending approvals:

- **In-app** - badge count on the approval queue icon
- **Email digest** - daily or weekly summary of pending items
- **Real-time** - get notified immediately for high-priority items
