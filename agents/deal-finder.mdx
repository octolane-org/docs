The Deal Finder Agent scans your email, calendar, and every connected data source to spot deals as they form - before you even realize there's an opportunity.

## What it does

The Deal Finder Agent continuously monitors:

- **Email** - detects buying signals, pricing discussions, demo requests, and inbound interest from your inbox
- **Calendar** - identifies meetings with potential customers and new prospects
- **250+ connected data sources** - web analytics, marketing tools, support tickets, and more
- **The web** - searches for signals about your prospects, like job changes, funding rounds, or expansion news

When it detects a deal, it automatically creates an opportunity in your pipeline with the right stage, associated contacts, and full context.

## How it finds deals

The Deal Finder doesn't just look for the word "deal" in your email. It understands conversational context:

- A prospect asking about pricing in an email thread → new opportunity detected
- A meeting titled "Product demo" with someone at a company you haven't talked to before → new opportunity detected
- A support conversation that shifts into an upsell discussion → new opportunity detected
- A web analytics signal showing a target account viewing your pricing page repeatedly → new opportunity detected

## What gets created

When a deal is detected, the agent creates:

- An **opportunity record** with a name, estimated stage, and source
- Links to the **associated contacts and company**
- A timeline of the **relevant emails, meetings, and interactions** that triggered the detection
- **Enrichment data** about the company - size, industry, funding, tech stack

## Staying in control

The Deal Finder creates opportunities based on real signals from your data. You can:

- Review and adjust deals it creates
- Set sensitivity levels - how strong a signal needs to be before a deal is created
- Define which data sources the agent should monitor
- Merge duplicates if the same deal is detected from multiple sources
