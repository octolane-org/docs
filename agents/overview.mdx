Octolane's agents aren't features you toggle on. They're employees you manage.

Each agent handles a specific job in your sales process. They talk to each other, hand off work, and escalate to you only when they need a human decision. You interact with them in plain English - not through configuration screens.

A customer put it best: *"You shouldn't call these Actions. You should call them Employees."*

## Meet your agents

| Agent | What it does | How you interact |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Signal Agent** | Finds companies visiting your website in real-time | Review identified companies in your pipeline |
| **Deal Finder Agent** | Detects deals from email, calendar, and 250+ data sources | New deals appear automatically in your pipeline |
| **Form Submission Agent** | Captures and qualifies every inbound form lead | Qualified leads are routed to the right stage |
| **Follow-Up Agent** | Drafts follow-up emails when conversations go quiet | Approve, edit, or dismiss from your queue |
| **Outreach Agent** | Sends personalized outreach to qualified prospects | Review and approve messages before they send |
| **Meeting Recorder** | Joins calls, transcribes, and extracts action items | Summaries and tasks appear on the deal record |
| **AI Chat** | Answers questions about your pipeline in plain English | Type any question into the chat bar |

## How agents coordinate

Agents don't work in silos. They pass work to each other like a real team:

- Signal Agent identifies a hot prospect → Deal Finder checks for existing email threads → Outreach Agent drafts a message
- Meeting Recorder captures action items from a call → Follow-Up Agent monitors if those items get addressed
- Form Submission Agent qualifies a new lead → Deal Finder creates an opportunity → Follow-Up Agent watches for engagement

This coordination happens automatically. You see the results in your pipeline and approval queue.

## Managing your agents

You manage agents the way you'd manage a team:

- **Turn agents on or off** - enable only the ones you need right now
- **Set their rules** - define what qualifies a lead, when to follow up, what tone to use
- **Review their work** - every action shows up in your approval queue before it goes out
- **Talk to them** - use AI Chat to ask questions, give instructions, or check on progress

Nothing happens without your say-so. Agents draft, suggest, and prepare - you approve.

## The approval queue

Every agent action that touches the outside world (sending an email, creating an outreach message, updating a deal stage) goes through your approval queue. 

Open it from the sidebar. Each item shows what the agent wants to do, why, and the full context. You approve, edit, or dismiss with one tap.
