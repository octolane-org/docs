The Signal Agent monitors your website traffic in real-time and identifies which companies are visiting - even if they never fill out a form or leave their contact info.

## What it does

When someone from a company visits your website, the Signal Agent:

1. **Identifies the company** - matches the visitor to a real business using IP intelligence and behavioral signals
2. **Enriches the record** - pulls in company size, industry, location, funding status, and key contacts
3. **Scores the visit** - evaluates how closely the company matches your ideal customer profile
4. **Creates or updates a record** - adds the company to your CRM or updates an existing one with the new activity
5. **Triggers next steps** - if the company qualifies, it can hand off to the Outreach Agent for automatic follow-up

## When to use it

The Signal Agent is for teams who want to know who's looking at their website before those visitors reach out. It's especially valuable when:

- You have meaningful website traffic but low form conversion rates
- You want to proactively reach out to companies researching your product
- You want to prioritize follow-ups based on which prospects are actively engaged on your site

## How to set it up

The Signal Agent requires the Signal tracking script to be installed on your website. See [Installing Signal](/signal/installing-signal) for the full setup guide.

Once the script is installed, the Signal Agent activates automatically. You can configure:

- **Qualification criteria** - which types of companies should be flagged as high priority
- **Handoff rules** - whether qualified companies should be automatically passed to the Outreach Agent
- **Notifications** - get alerted when high-value companies visit your site

## What you'll see

In your Octolane dashboard, the Signal Agent surfaces:

- A list of companies currently visiting or recently visited your website
- Which pages they viewed and how many times they've been back
- How well each company matches your ideal customer profile
- Suggested contacts at those companies for outreach
