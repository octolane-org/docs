Octolane gives you real-time visibility into your pipeline health, deal velocity, agent performance, and revenue forecasts - without building reports manually.

## Dashboard overview

Your reporting dashboard shows:

- **Pipeline value** - total value of open deals by stage
- **Deal velocity** - average time deals spend in each stage
- **Win rate** - percentage of deals that close successfully
- **Activity volume** - emails sent, meetings held, follow-ups completed
- **Agent performance** - how many actions each agent has taken, approval rates, and outcomes

## Reports you can generate

Ask AI Chat to generate reports in plain English:

- "How many deals did we close this month?"
- "What's our average deal size for enterprise accounts?"
- "Show me pipeline conversion rates by stage"
- "Which rep has the most stale deals?"
- "Compare this quarter's pipeline to last quarter"

## At-risk deals

Octolane automatically flags deals that show signs of stalling - no recent email activity, missed follow-ups, or declining engagement. These appear in a dedicated "At Risk" view so you can take action before deals go cold.

## Team performance

If you have a team, reporting includes per-rep breakdowns: deals created, meetings booked, response times, follow-up rates, and close rates. This helps you spot coaching opportunities and replicate what your top performers do well.
