Find anything in your CRM instantly. Save custom views for the data you check often, filter by any field, and search across deals, contacts, companies, emails, and notes.

## Saved views

Create saved views for the data you look at regularly:

- "My open deals" - all deals owned by you that aren't closed
- "Stale deals" - deals with no activity in the last 14 days
- "Enterprise prospects" - companies with 500+ employees
- "Hot leads this week" - contacts who visited the website and opened an email

Go to any list page (deals, contacts, companies), apply filters, and click **Save View**. Your saved views appear in the sidebar for quick access.

## Filters

Filter any list by:

- Standard fields - stage, owner, value, date created, last activity
- Custom fields - any field you've added to your data model, including AI fields
- Activity - last email sent, last meeting, number of interactions
- Signal data - website visits, pages viewed, visit frequency

Combine multiple filters with AND/OR logic to create precise segments.

## Search

The global search bar searches across everything - deals, contacts, companies, emails, meeting transcripts, notes, and agent activity. Type any keyword, name, or phrase to find it.

You can also use [AI Chat](/agents/ai-chat) for natural language searches: "Show me all deals over $10K that haven't had activity this week."
