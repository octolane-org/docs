After every call recorded by the [Meeting Recorder](/agents/meeting-recorder), Octolane generates a structured summary with action items and follow-up tasks. You get the full picture without taking a single note during the meeting.

## What you get after every meeting

- **Plain-English summary** - a concise recap of what was discussed, key decisions, and open questions
- **Action items** - specific next steps extracted from the conversation, assigned to the right person
- **Follow-up tasks** - suggested tasks based on what was discussed
- **Full transcript** - a complete, speaker-labeled transcript of the meeting
- **Key moments** - timestamps for important topics like pricing, objections, or commitments

## Where meeting notes live

Meeting notes are attached to the relevant deal, contact, and company records. They show up in the Activity Feed with a link to the full summary and transcript.

## Using meeting notes

Meeting notes feed into the rest of Octolane's system:

- The **Follow-Up Agent** uses them to draft context-aware follow-up emails
- **Custom AI Fields** can extract specific data points from transcripts (e.g., "What budget did they mention?")
- **AI Chat** can answer questions about past meetings (e.g., "What did we discuss with Acme last Tuesday?")
