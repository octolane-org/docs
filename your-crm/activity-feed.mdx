The Activity Feed is a single, chronological timeline of everything that happens in your CRM. Every email, meeting, agent action, website visit, note, and deal update - all in one place.

## Where you'll find it

Activity feeds appear in three places:

- **Deal page** - shows all activity related to a specific deal
- **Contact page** - shows all activity related to a specific person
- **Company page** - shows all activity related to a specific company

There's also a global activity feed accessible from the sidebar that shows recent activity across your entire CRM.

## What shows up

- Emails sent and received
- Meetings scheduled and completed
- Agent actions - follow-ups drafted, outreach sent, deals created
- Website visits (via Signal)
- Notes added (by you or by AI)
- Deal stage changes
- Contact and company enrichment updates
- Form submissions

## Filtering activity

Use filters to narrow the feed by activity type, date range, team member, or agent. This is useful for reviewing what happened with a specific deal last week or seeing all agent actions in the past 24 hours.
