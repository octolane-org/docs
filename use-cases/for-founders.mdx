You're building the product, talking to customers, raising money, and managing the team. You don't have time to update a CRM - and you probably can't afford to hire a sales rep yet.

Octolane gives you a sales team without hiring one.

## What Octolane does for you as a founder

**Finds your prospects.** Signal identifies companies visiting your website. The Deal Finder detects opportunities from your inbox. You stop guessing who's interested and start seeing it in your pipeline.

**Qualifies automatically.** Every lead gets scored. You know who's worth your time without manually researching every company.

**Follows up for you.** The Follow-Up Agent monitors every conversation and drafts follow-ups when things go quiet. You approve with one tap. No more deals dying because you were busy with something else.

**Keeps your pipeline clean.** Contacts are enriched, deals are updated, and activity is logged - all without you typing into fields. Your pipeline reflects reality, not what you remembered to enter last Friday.

## Typical founder setup

1. Connect your Gmail and Google Calendar (2 minutes)
2. Install Signal on your website (5 minutes)
3. Connect any other tools you use - LinkedIn, WhatsApp, analytics
4. Let Octolane build your pipeline from your existing conversations

Within an hour, you'll have a pipeline populated with real deals, enriched contacts, and a clear view of what needs your attention.

## Why founders choose Octolane over HubSpot or Pipedrive

Traditional CRMs require you to do the work - log calls, update fields, create deals. Octolane does that work for you. It's the difference between a spreadsheet that waits for input and a system that actively runs your sales motion.

For a solo founder, that difference is easily 10+ hours a week saved.
