Your reps should be selling, not updating Salesforce. Octolane eliminates the CRM busywork so your team spends time on what actually closes deals - conversations with customers.

## What Octolane does for sales teams

**Eliminates manual data entry.** Deals, contacts, and activity are created and updated automatically from email, calendar, and your connected tools. Reps never need to log a call or update a field again.

**Prospects for your team.** Signal finds companies on your website. The Deal Finder spots opportunities in your team's inboxes. The Outreach Agent drafts personalized messages. Your reps get qualified leads delivered to them.

**Never misses a follow-up.** The Follow-Up Agent watches every conversation across your team. When threads go quiet, it drafts follow-ups in each rep's voice and queues them for approval.

**Gives managers real-time visibility.** AI Reporting shows pipeline health, deal velocity, rep activity, and at-risk deals - updated in real-time from actual activity, not self-reported data.

## Team setup

1. Connect every rep's email and calendar
2. Install Signal on your website
3. Define your pipeline stages and qualification criteria
4. Set up agent rules - when to follow up, what qualifies a lead, how outreach should sound

Octolane merges data across your team automatically. When two reps email the same company, Octolane creates one unified account record.

## Manager benefits

- See which deals are actually progressing vs. which ones reps say are progressing
- Get accurate forecasts based on real activity, not gut feeling
- Spot coaching opportunities - which reps follow up consistently, which ones let deals go stale
- Reduce ramp time for new hires - Octolane handles the CRM learning curve
