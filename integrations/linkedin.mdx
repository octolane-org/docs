Connect LinkedIn to Octolane to enrich contact data, track prospect activity, and keep your network in your CRM.

## What the integration does

- **Enriches contacts** with LinkedIn profile data - job title, company, location, headline, connections
- **Syncs prospect data** - bring LinkedIn connections and saved leads into your CRM
- **Tracks activity** - see which prospects are engaging with your LinkedIn content

## Setting it up

Go to **Settings → Integrations → LinkedIn** and follow the connection flow. Once connected, Octolane starts enriching existing contacts with LinkedIn data and can import new ones.

## Using LinkedIn with your agents

LinkedIn data feeds into your agents' work:

- The **Outreach Agent** can reference LinkedIn profile details when drafting personalized messages
- **AI Enrichment** pulls LinkedIn data to fill in job titles, companies, and professional context
- **Custom AI Fields** can extract LinkedIn-specific data points
