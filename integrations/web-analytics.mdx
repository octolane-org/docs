Connect your web analytics tools to Octolane so your CRM knows which pages prospects are viewing, how they found your website, and what content they engage with.

## What it adds to your CRM

- **Page-level visitor data** - see which specific pages a prospect viewed, not just that they visited your site
- **Referral sources** - understand whether prospects came from search, social, ads, or direct traffic
- **Engagement metrics** - time on page, pages per session, and return visits
- **Conversion tracking** - track which pages and campaigns lead to form submissions and deals

## How it works with Signal

Web analytics data enriches what Signal already captures. While Signal identifies which companies are visiting, web analytics adds depth - showing exactly what they looked at and how engaged they were.

## Setting it up

Go to **Settings → Integrations** and connect your web analytics tool. Data starts flowing into contact and company records within minutes.
