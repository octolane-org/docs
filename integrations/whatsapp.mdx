Many deals happen over WhatsApp - especially in markets where messaging is the primary business communication channel. The WhatsApp integration brings those conversations into Octolane so nothing falls through the cracks.

## What it does

- **Syncs conversations** - WhatsApp messages appear in the Activity Feed on the relevant contact and deal records
- **Creates contacts** - new WhatsApp conversations can automatically create contact records
- **Powers agents** - the Deal Finder Agent can detect deals from WhatsApp conversations, and the Follow-Up Agent can monitor WhatsApp threads for stale conversations

## Setting it up

Go to **Settings → Integrations → WhatsApp** and follow the connection flow.
