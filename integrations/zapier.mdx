Zapier connects Octolane to over 5,000 apps, letting you automate data flows and trigger actions across your entire tool stack.

## What you can do

**Triggers** (when something happens in Octolane):
- New deal created
- Deal stage changed
- New contact added
- Form submission received
- Signal identifies a new company

**Actions** (do something in Octolane):
- Create a deal
- Create or update a contact
- Add a note to a record
- Update a deal stage

## Example automations

- When a new deal is created in Octolane → send a Slack notification to your sales channel
- When a lead fills out a Typeform → create a contact in Octolane
- When a deal moves to "Closed Won" → create an invoice in Stripe
- When a new contact is added in Octolane → add them to a Mailchimp audience

## Setting it up

1. Go to [zapier.com](https://zapier.com) and search for "Octolane"
2. Choose a trigger or action
3. Connect your Octolane account via API key (found in **Settings → Integrations → Zapier**)
4. Build your automation and turn it on
