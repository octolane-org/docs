Octolane connects to over 250 tools and data sources your business already uses. Every integration feeds data into your CRM automatically - no manual syncing, no CSV exports, no copy-pasting between tools.

## Why this matters

Traditional CRMs only know what you tell them. Octolane knows what's happening across your entire business because it's connected to the tools where work actually happens.

When a lead opens a marketing email, visits your pricing page, submits a support ticket, or messages you on WhatsApp - Octolane sees it and updates the relevant records automatically.

## How connections work

Octolane uses a combination of native integrations, MCP (Model Context Protocol) connections, and Zapier to connect with your tools. Most integrations take under a minute to set up:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations**
2. Find the tool you want to connect
3. Click **Connect** and authorize access
4. Data starts flowing immediately

## Categories of integrations

- **Email & Calendar** - Gmail, Google Calendar (with more providers coming soon)
- **Social & Messaging** - LinkedIn, WhatsApp
- **Web Analytics** - connect your analytics tools so Octolane knows which pages prospects are viewing
- **Marketing** - email marketing platforms, ad tools, landing page builders
- **Support** - helpdesk and customer support tools
- **Automation** - Zapier for connecting to thousands of additional apps

## Requesting an integration

Don't see the tool you need? Contact us at [help@octolane.com](mailto:help@octolane.com) or request it through **Settings → Integrations → Request Integration**.
