## February 17, 2025

### Report Builder V1

We updated the dashboard with reports, deeper insights, and richer data points!

**What's new?**
- ✅ Better visibility into your pipeline
- 📈 Track lead capture trends over time
- ⚙️ Analyze workflow impact on pipeline creation
- 💰 Identify stalled deals & track ARR closed
- 📅 Measure outbound efforts & follow-up effectiveness
- 🌍 See lead sources & which pages drive conversions

Head over to the dashboard to see it in action! Explore reports, uncover insights, and optimize your pipeline—all in one place.

### Form Builder V1

Octolane Form Builder, but better—now LIVE! We just made forms smarter, smoother, and more flexible to help you capture leads effortlessly.

**What's new?**
- ✅ More fields with better validation
- ✅ Drag & Drop – the most requested feature is here!
- ✅ Mobile & Desktop Preview
- ✅ Custom Logo Upload
- ✅ Smoother customization 

Head over to the Forms dashboard to build, customize, and optimize your forms in seconds!

## February 10, 2025

### File Management V1

You can now upload and organize files in Octolane! Supported file types include PDFs for now and more are coming. Create folders to manage them efficiently—everything is automatically mapped to the related account, contact, or opportunity.

To manage files and folders, click on any record from the table or open an individual record page. From there, you can upload, organize, and control files and folders.

### Activity V1
<iframe  width="640" height="360" src="https://www.loom.com/embed/d1d3c0c73ab749839faa48dfd409a895?sid=89df9a97-4c17-4ebc-b201-a598b3ddb854" frameborder="0" webkitallowfullscreen mozallowfullscreen allowfullscreen></iframe>


Introducing the new Activity Panel! Now, you can view the complete history of emails and meetings for any contact, account, or opportunity in one place.

To check the activity of any record, click on a record from the table or open its individual page. From there, you can see upcoming events, past email interactions, and meetings. Click on any interaction to view more details.
