---
title: "Inbound from website"
description: "Signal → AI scoring → outreach → deal."
---

Convert anonymous website traffic into pipeline.

1. **Install Signal**. One snippet on your site.
2. **Define ICP filters**. Signal surfaces only companies that match.
3. **AI scoring**. Each visit scored on fit + intent (pages viewed, return visits).
4. **AI-drafted outreach**. Personalized openers referencing what they read.
5. **Approve and send**. Drafts queue for review.
6. **Auto-create deal on reply**. First reply triggers an opportunity in your pipeline.
