---
title: "Working through your approval queue"
description: "How to run the approval queue without it piling up."
---

## Rules

1. **Same time, every day.** 15 minutes morning, 15 minutes evening. Calendar block it.
2. **Decide every item.** Approve, edit, or reject. Never leave it.
3. **Use `A` / `R`.** Approve-next and reject-next keyboard shortcuts.
4. **Tune thresholds weekly.** Items you almost always approve → auto-approve at high confidence.
5. **Reject with reason.** The system learns from your rejection reasons.

## When the queue is overflowing

- Filter by action type and bulk-approve the safe ones (enrichment, field updates).
- Move email drafts to a separate dedicated session.
- Raise auto-approve thresholds for low-risk items.
