A four-week sprint to fill the top of your funnel and book 50 qualified demos.

## Week 1: list + ICP

- Define ICP precisely (industry, headcount, tech stack, signal)
- Build a list of 500 fitting accounts (Signal + import + manual)

## Week 2: outreach

- AI-drafted personalized outreach, 100/day
- Approve everything; track reply rate by message variant

## Week 3: book

- Triple-touch sequence on no-reply
- Calendar links in every approved follow-up

## Week 4: run

- Meeting bot on every call
- Approval queue every morning to advance stages and queue next steps
