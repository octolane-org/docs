---
title: "Your first 7 days"
description: "The retention checklist."
---

Users who hit 50 deals in their first week stay on Octolane past 6 months at 3x the rate of those who don't.

## Day 1
- Connect Gmail + Calendar
- Confirm initial sync completed

## Day 2
- Import any list from your old CRM or spreadsheet
- Run merge-duplicates pass

## Day 3
- Define 3 AI fields
- Build your pipeline (5 stages max)

## Day 4
- Run your first AI Chat report: *"Pipeline by stage."*
- Approve everything in the queue

## Day 5
- Install Signal on your site
- Turn on meeting bot

## Day 6
- Invite teammates (or don't. Solo works)
- Build 3 saved views

## Day 7
- Forecast: *"This quarter, commit and best case."*
- Audit AI field accuracy; tune prompts
