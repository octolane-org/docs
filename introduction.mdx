Octolane is a self-driving CRM that keeps itself updated by reading the emails and meetings your team already has in Gmail and Google Calendar-no more manual fields to fill, no more stale pipelines.

Octolane AI positions itself as an "What AI Salesforce should have been," moving beyond the conventional system of records to become a system of actions. This means the platform doesn't just store customer data but actively predicts and executes actions to move the sales processes.

## Why teams pick Octolane

- Save 10+ hours every week-no more manual logging
- Never miss a deal update-emails and meetings auto-sync to the right record
- Automatic contact enrichment-fresh titles, companies, and signals without data vendors
- Built to be hands-off-AI does the busywork while you keep selling
