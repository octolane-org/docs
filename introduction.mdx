Octolane AI, a Y Combinator-backed startup, specializes in handling and analyzing vast datasets, ranging from healthcare to sales. Our first public product is on providing real-time, up-to-date B2B data enrichment solutions powered by large language models (LLMs). We manage over 50 million company data and 300 million contact data entries, constantly growing and updating to ensure precision and accuracy. Our proprietory AI/ML applications are designed to process millions of requests monthly, offering seamless integration for your business needs.
