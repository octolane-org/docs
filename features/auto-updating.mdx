# How auto-updating works

Octolane reads the signals your team already generates—emails and meetings—then writes structured updates back to your CRM without anyone touching a field.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ingest" icon="inbox">
    We ingest Gmail threads and Calendar events with least-privilege scopes, focusing on metadata, participants, and relevant snippets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Understand" icon="brain">
    LLMs classify intent (new lead, follow-up, risk, next step), extract contacts, companies, dates, amounts, and owners.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Write back" icon="sparkles">
    We map signals to existing accounts/contacts/opportunities and update fields, notes, and timelines automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="What updates automatically" icon="bolt">
    - New contacts with enriched firmographics  
    - Meeting + email activity on the right record  
    - Next steps and follow-up dates  
    - Opportunity stages and amounts when detected
  </Card>
  <Card title="Benefits" icon="clock">
    Clean data without manual entry, fresher timelines, and zero missed follow-ups.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
