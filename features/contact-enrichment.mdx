# Contact enrichment

Octolane enriches every contact we see in Gmail or Calendar so you never chase down titles, companies, or LinkedIn profiles manually.

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="What we capture" icon="user">
    - Name, title, company, seniority  
    - Email and domain intelligence  
    - Signals from meetings and replies
  </Card>
  <Card title="How it stays clean" icon="broom">
    We dedupe against existing contacts, prefer verified fields, and refresh on every new interaction.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where enrichment is applied" icon="map">
    Contacts are linked to the right account and opportunity automatically, so activity history is instantly usable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why it matters" icon="chart-line">
    Your team gets up-to-date org charts, champions, and buying signals without extra tools or exports.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
