---
title: "How deals are auto-detected from email"
description: "The logic behind deal detection."
---

Octolane reads your outbound and inbound email and flags conversations that look like deals based on:

- Buying-intent language ("pricing", "demo", "trial", "contract")
- New external contacts at a target-profile company
- Calendar invites that follow within 7 days
- Reply cadence consistent with sales motion

Detected deals appear in your [approval queue](/ai/approvals) for one-click confirm. Auto-approve thresholds are configurable.

See [Auto-detecting deals from email](/ai/auto-detecting-deals).
