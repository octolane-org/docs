Quick reference for the terminology used across Octolane.

- **Account**. A company record. Auto-created from emails, meetings, and form submissions.
- **Contact**. A person record, linked to an Account.
- **Opportunity**. A deal record with a stage, amount, and close date. Auto-detected from email.
- **Task**. A first-class to-do with an owner, due date, and completion state.
- **Pipeline**. An ordered set of stages a deal moves through.
- **Stage**. A step in a pipeline with entry/exit criteria and a probability.
- **AI field**. A custom field that fills itself in by reading your conversations.
- **Signal**. A real-time identification of a company visiting your website.
- **View**. A saved filter + columns + sort across any object.
- **Activity**. Any email, meeting, note, or AI action on a record's timeline.
- **Approval queue**. Where AI-drafted actions wait for your review.
- **Custom object**. A new record type beyond Accounts, Contacts, and Opportunities (Team plan).
