Octolane is a self-driving CRM. It connects to Gmail and Calendar, then runs your sales pipeline automatically. Auto-detected deals, auto-filled fields, auto-drafted follow-ups, auto-recorded meetings. You stay in control through a human-in-the-loop approval queue.

## Who it's for

- **Founder-led B2B teams** running their first sales motion.
- **Seed–Series A startups** that don't want to spend their first year configuring Salesforce.
- **Solo founders** doing 50–500 conversations a quarter who refuse to lose deals to bad data entry.

## What to expect

- **10 minutes to first value.** Connect Gmail and Calendar, see auto-detected deals appear.
- **Plain English everywhere.** AI Chat is the front door. Ask for any report, command any change.
- **Human in the loop by default.** Nothing consequential happens without your approval. Tune thresholds when you trust it.
