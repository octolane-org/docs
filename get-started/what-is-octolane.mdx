Octolane is the world's first self-driving CRM. It doesn't wait for your sales team to log calls, update fields, or chase down follow-ups. Instead, Octolane connects to your email, calendar, website, web analytics, and over 250 other data sources - and runs your sales pipeline automatically.

Every deal, every contact, every follow-up, every piece of prospect data - handled by agents that work 24/7.

## The problem with every other CRM

Traditional CRMs like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive are databases that depend on humans to type data in. Your sales team spends hours every week updating deal stages, logging calls, and entering contact information. Data goes stale fast. Follow-ups get missed. Deals slip through the cracks - not because your team is bad, but because the CRM doesn't do anything on its own.

Even newer CRMs that call themselves "AI-powered" still wait for you to log calls, update fields, and decide when to follow up. They might suggest a next step, but they won't take it.

## How Octolane is different

Octolane is not a database you update. It's a sales organization you manage.

Octolane's agents work like dedicated employees. Each one handles a specific job:

- **Signal Agent** - finds companies visiting your website in real-time
- **Deal Finder Agent** - detects new deals from your email, calendar, and 250+ data sources.
- **Form Submission Agent** - captures and qualifies every inbound lead
- **Follow-Up Agent** - writes and sends follow-ups in your voice when conversations go quiet
- **Outreach Agent** - reaches out to qualified prospects automatically
- **Meeting Recorder** - joins your calls, transcribes, and pulls out action items

These agents coordinate with each other. When Signal identifies a company on your site, it can hand that lead to the Outreach Agent to send a personalized message. When the Follow-Up Agent notices a deal has gone quiet, it drafts a reply in your tone and queues it for your approval.

You manage them the way you'd manage a team: in plain English, through a single approval queue.

## Who Octolane is for

Octolane is built for founders, sales teams, and agencies who want clean pipeline data and proactive sales execution without hiring more people or spending hours on CRM busywork.

- **Solo founders** who need a sales team but can't afford one yet
- **Sales teams** who want to sell instead of updating Salesforce
- **Agencies** managing multiple client pipelines simultaneously

## What makes Octolane self-driving

1. **It finds your prospects.** Signal identifies companies visiting your website. The Deal Finder scans your email, calendar, and connected tools for new opportunities. Octolane constantly searches the web for relevant signals about your prospects and market.

2. **It qualifies automatically.** Every lead gets scored against your ideal customer profile. Form submissions are captured and routed instantly. No manual review needed.

3. **It follows up for you.** When conversations stall, the Follow-Up Agent drafts emails that match your writing style and tone. You approve with one tap.

4. **It keeps your data clean.** Contacts and companies are automatically enriched with job titles, company size, funding data, and social profiles - pulled from the web in real-time, not a stale database.

5. **It connects to everything.** Over 250 data sources including email, calendar, web analytics, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and thousands more through Zapier.
