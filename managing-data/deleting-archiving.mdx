---
title: "Deleting and archiving"
description: "How deletion works, what's recoverable."
---

- **Archive**. Hides a record from views but keeps history. Reversible anytime.
- **Delete**. Soft-deletes for 30 days, then permanent.

Bulk deletes are undoable for 24 hours. Workspace owners can restore from the trash within 30 days.
