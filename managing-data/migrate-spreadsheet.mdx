The fastest migration in the industry. Because Octolane fills in the gaps.

2. [Import](/managing-data/importing). Even messy column names map well.
3. Connect Gmail and Calendar. Octolane backfills enrichment, activity, and missing fields from your history.
4. Run [Merging duplicates](/managing-data/merging-duplicates) once.

Done.
