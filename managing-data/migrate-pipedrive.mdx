---
title: "Migrating from Pipedrive"
description: "Step-by-step switch from Pipedrive."
---

1. Export Organizations, People, Deals, Activities, and Notes as CSV from Pipedrive.
2. Connect Gmail and Calendar.
3. [Import each CSV](/managing-data/importing). Pipeline stages map directly.
4. Re-create any custom fields.

Most Pipedrive workspaces migrate in under an hour.
