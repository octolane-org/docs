Three plans. Pick the one that matches how you sell.

## Pro. \$49/seat/month

For founders and small teams running their first real sales motion.

- Gmail + Calendar sync
- Auto-detected deals, AI fields, AI Chat
- Meeting recorder
- Signal (website visitor identification)
- AI enrichment
- Zapier
- REST API and MCP server

## Business. \$99/seat/month

For growing teams that need more control and collaboration.

- Everything in Pro
- Slack app (beta). Octolane in every channel
- Advanced approval queue and auto-approve thresholds
- Multiple pipelines
- Scheduled exports
- SSO
- Priority support

## Team. Custom

For teams with custom data models, complex permissions, or volume.

- Everything in Business
- Custom objects
- SCIM provisioning
- Dedicated success manager
- Annual contract

[Contact sales](mailto:sales@octolane.com) for Team plan pricing.

## Managing your plan

Open **Settings → Billing** to view your current plan, upgrade, downgrade, or update payment methods. See [Billing](/account/billing).

## Free trial

New workspaces get a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. Pick any plan at the end of the trial.
