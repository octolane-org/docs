Octolane offers three plans to fit teams of any size.

## Free

Get started at no cost.
- Connect 1 email account
- Basic pipeline management
- Limited agent actions per month

## Starter - $10/month per seat

For individuals and small teams getting started with a self-driving CRM.
- Connect multiple email accounts
- All agents included
- Signal website visitor tracking
- AI enrichment
- Zapier integration

## Pro - $19/month per seat

For growing teams that want the full self-driving experience.
- Everything in Starter
- Custom AI Fields
- AI Reporting
- Advanced agent configuration
- Priority support

## Managing your plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** to view your current plan, upgrade, downgrade, or manage payment methods.

## Free trial

New workspaces get a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose to continue on any plan.
