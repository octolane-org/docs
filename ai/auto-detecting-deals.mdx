---
title: "Auto-detecting deals from email"
description: "From 'looks like a deal' to opportunity record."
---

Octolane scans your sent and received mail for buying-intent patterns. When a thread crosses the threshold, it proposes an opportunity with:

- Account (auto-created if new)
- Primary contact
- Estimated stage based on conversation depth
- Suggested amount when one has been mentioned

One click to accept. See [How deals are auto-detected from email](/email-calendar/deal-auto-detection).
