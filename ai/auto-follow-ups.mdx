---
title: "Auto follow-ups"
description: "When and how Octolane drafts follow-up emails."
---

When a deal goes quiet past its expected reply window, Octolane drafts a follow-up that:

- References the last conversation specifically
- Matches your prior tone and length
- Offers a clear next step

Drafts queue in your [approval queue](/ai/approvals). Approve, edit, or skip.
