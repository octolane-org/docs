---
title: "How Octolane's AI works"
description: "The mental model: detect, draft, enrich, approve."
---

Four primitives:

1. **Detect**. Surface deals, contacts, tasks, and field values from your conversations.
2. **Draft**. Write follow-ups, outreach, and meeting notes.
3. **Enrich**. Pull external data (job titles, company size, funding, tech stack).
4. **Approve**. Every consequential action queues for your one-click review.

Set auto-approve thresholds when you trust the output enough to skip review.
