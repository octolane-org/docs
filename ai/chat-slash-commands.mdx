---
title: "AI Chat and slash commands"
description: "Skills, slash commands, and the AI Chat composer."
---

Open AI Chat with `Cmd+/`. Type freely, or use slash commands:

- `/find`. Search any object
- `/create`. Opportunity, task, contact, account, note
- `/report`. Generate a report inline
- `/email`. Draft an email to a contact or thread
- `/forecast`. Pipeline forecast
- `/explain`. Explain why an AI field has its current value
