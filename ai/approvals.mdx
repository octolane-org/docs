---
title: "Approvals and human-in-the-loop"
description: "Review queue, auto-approve thresholds, and overrides."
---

Every AI action that changes data or sends a message queues for review.

- **Approve**. Applies the change.
- **Edit**. Change before applying.
- **Reject**. Octolane learns from rejections.

Set auto-approve thresholds per action type and confidence level once you trust the output. Workspace owners can disable auto-approve globally.
