## Pipeline health

> "Show open pipeline by stage with total amount and deal count. Flag any deal with no activity in the last 14 days."

## Deal velocity

> "Average days in each stage for deals closed in the last 90 days."

## Forecast

> "Forecast for this quarter based on weighted pipeline and historical close rate per stage."

## Win/loss

> "Win rate by source over the last two quarters. Break down lost deals by reason."

## Activity per rep

> "Emails sent, meetings held, and deals advanced by rep this week."
