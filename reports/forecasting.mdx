---

Octolane's forecast doesn't trust your stage assignments alone. It re-scores every deal using:

- Recency and depth of conversations
- AI-extracted decision criteria, blockers, and timelines
- Historical win rate of similar deals in your workspace

Ask AI Chat: *"Forecast this quarter, commit and best case."*
